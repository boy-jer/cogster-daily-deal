Cogster Merchant/Organization Loyalty ProjectAgreement

This Merchant Registration Agreement is made and entered into between you and Local Markets, Inc., a Pennsylvania corporation d/b/a Cogster ("Cogster").

The Cogster marketplace is a person-to-person online pre-paid goods and services Platform (the "Platform") operated by Cogster for the registration of Merchants and Members, the receipt, display and matching of listings and offers for pre-paid goods and services and the origination and sale of those pre-paid goods and services. The following Agreement describes the services and your rights and obligations should you elect to register as a Merchant on the Platform. Cogster is referred to in this Agreement as "we" or "us."

  1. Registration as a Cogster Merchant. The Cogster marketplace is a person-to-person online pre-paid goods and services Platform (the "Platform") operated by Cogster for the registration of Merchants and Members, the receipt, display and matching of listings and offers for pre-paid goods and services and the origination and sale of those pre-paid goods and services. The following Agreement describes the services and your rights and obligations should you elect to register as a Merchant on the Platform. Cogster is referred to in this Agreement as "we" or "us."

    We reserve the right to restrict access to the Platform to individuals who meet minimum credit guidelines and other criteria, as determined by us in our sole discretion.

  2. Listings. You offer pre-paid goods and services by posting a listing on the Platform. The minimum and maximum amounts you may request are posted on the Cogster website and are subject to change by us at any time without notice. To post a listing, you may provide the amount of the funds you are requesting.

    Cogster Members may ask you questions about your listing and you may, but are not required to, respond to such questions. If you do elect to respond to a Member's question you may respond privately, or you may elect to have the question and answer posted publicly in the listing. Members' questions are not posted in the listing or displayed elsewhere on our website unless you elect to answer the question and elect to make the question and answer publicly available, in which case the question and answer appears in the listing.

    Merchant listings are displayed on the Platform. This means that people who visit the Cogster website will be able to view your listing. Upon your submission of a listing, you authorize Cogster to display the listing on the Platform for purposes of offering pre-paid goods and services and for enabling Cogster Members who have purchased your pre-paid goods or services to offer such goods or services to other Members at any time while your obligation is outstanding. You authorize and agree that Cogster may obtain and display updated information from your credit file with one or more consumer credit reporting agencies, as well as information about the payment history and status of your obligation to provide pre-paid goods and services, on the Platform at any time or times that your obligation is offered for sale by Members holding such obligations. To facilitate acceptance for your listing or for listings posted by Members offering to sell such obligations, Cogster may forward the listing by email to third parties, including but not limited to registered Cogster Members, and may display the listing in promotional, advertising and marketing materials, and you authorize Cogster to do so.

    You authorize Cogster to verify your business location, income, line of business and any other information you provide in connection with a listing or your registration as a Merchant, and you agree that Cogster may contact third parties to verify information you provide. If such information changes after you post a listing but before the listing expires, you must either (i) promptly notify Cogster of the change, or (ii) withdraw your listing. If you elect to withdraw your listing as a result of a change, you may (but are not required to) post a new listing containing the updated information. You cannot edit or amend your listing once it is posted on the Platform; however, you have the right to withdraw your listing at any time prior to expiration of the listing, and you may post another listing if you desire. Cogster reserves the right, in its sole discretion, to limit the number of listings you post or attempt to post on the Platform.

    Your listing is a request for the sale of pre-paid goods and services in the amount specified in the listing. In order to post a listing on the Platform, you must have good faith intent to provide the pre-paid goods or services and your listing must be consistent with that intent.

    You have the right to withdraw your listing at any time prior to expiration of the listing. If your listing receives a Member offer in, or Member offers totaling, the amount of your requested funding, you may receive funding from Cogster in the amount you requested, subject to our right to verify information you provided in connection with your listing and your registration as a Cogster user and our other rights as described in Sections 3 and 6, below. If funding is made to you, you do not have any right to rescind the obligation.

    Number of Listings. Cogster reserves the right, in its sole discretion, to limit the number of listings you post or attempt to post on the Platform. You may have only one listing outstanding at a time.

    Duration of Listings. When you post a listing, your listing will be displayed on the Platform for thirty (30) days or for such other time period that we may in our sole discretion establish and amend from time to time. However, if your listing receives a Member offer in, or Member offers totaling, the full amount of your requested Memberships prior to expiration of your listing, you may elect to end your listing early - you need not wait until your listing expires. When you post a listing, it will be displayed on the Platform along with all other listings until you end the listing or the listing expires, or until the listing is withdrawn by you or by us as provided in Section 3 below.

    Prohibited Activities. You agree that you will not, in connection with any listings or other transactions involving or potentially involving Cogster, (i) make any false, misleading or deceptive statements or omissions of material fact in your listing; (ii) offer illegal goods or services; (iii) misrepresent your identity, or describe, present or portray yourself as a person other than yourself, whether in a narrative description or a photo in your listing; or (iv) represent yourself to any person as a director, officer or employee of Cogster, unless you are such director, officer or employee.

  4. Matching of Offers and Listings; Funding.
    • Registered Cogster Members will be able to review your listing and may commit funds to purchase, in various amounts, pre-paid goods and services to be provided by you. This means that the Cogster Members will receive pre-paid goods and services. You acknowledge and agree that a Member's commitment to purchase pre-paid goods and services corresponding to all or a portion of your funding from us does not confer any rights to you. You also acknowledge and agree that Cogster Members make their own decisions whether to commit funds.
    • Cogster's Platform will automatically match your listing with any Member offers to purchase pre-paid goods and services. Most listings are matched with multiple Member offers.
    • A match of your listing with one or more offers in the full amount of your requested funding results in funding from Cogster to you, subject to our right to verify information as provided in this Agreement. Proceeds are disbursed into your designated deposit account. Cogster will service all funding made to Cogster Merchant Members through the Platform.
    • We do not warrant or guaranty that your listing will be matched with any Member offers. Your listing must receive Member offers totaling the entire amount of your requested funding in order for funding to be made.

  8. Providing Pre-Paid Goods and Services. At the time you register as a Merchant, you must provide your deposit account information to facilitate transfers of funds to and from your deposit account. Cogster will act as the servicer for all Memberships, and all communications regarding your funding must be made to Cogster. You agree to honor certificates issued on your behalf by Cogster. Cogster will issue certificates, in a form determined exclusively by Cogster, to contributing Members in the amount of the particular purchasing Option (the “Option”) you list and Member selects. The first Option, Option A, includes: for each $10 unit purchased by a Member, the Member receives from Cogster and you agree to honor: one $10 certificate, awarded within the first 30 days of each Member’s original date of purchase, one additional $5 certificate between days 31 and 60, counted from each Member’s original date of purchase, and one additional $5 certificate, between days 61 and 90, counted from each Member’s original date of purchase, with each certificate having a redemption period of 30 days from the date of its issuance. Cogster will determine the method the certificate is delivered to the Members (e.g., paper copy, electronically or via a Membership card). You are responsible for tracking the gift certificates you receive. Cogster’s only obligation is to provide you with notice of the gift certificates Cogster has distributed. Cogster is not responsible for counterfeit or unauthorized certificate use, and Cogster recommends that you record gift certificates accepted in order to prevent unauthorized certificates. You agree to treat the certificates as cash, and to not put limits on their use, such as blackout dates or restrictions on the purchase of discounted/special/sale items, etc. A second Option, Option B, may be selected by you, if available on the website, and include a somewhat different number of days and a different number of certificates, with differing values. Provided you opt to post a listing under Option B, you agree to honor the certificates, their amounts, over the number of days you select.
